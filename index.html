Welcome to Melon Tunes

Tired of not knowing how people mysteriously tap on watermelons and magically proclaim that they've found the right one?

Well oh boy do we have the project for you.

At Melon Tunes, we tell you whether or not that watermelon is right for you!

How it Works

Data Collection

To kickstart this project, we knocked-on, observed, assessed, and enjoyed 50 watermelons.

Model

Selecting and analyzing certain features let us create a decision tree-based model that tells us whether or not we expect a new watermelon with certain features to taste and feel a certain way.

Improvement

Every time you buy a watermelon is a chance to make this project better. Simply record the features of the watermelon before cutting into it, rate the attributes after cutting into it, and submit the form with your findings. We'll periodically incorporate new watermelons' data into the model's training dataset.
